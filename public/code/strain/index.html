strain library

April 1, 2019
used canonical synthetic biology techniques to engineer a small library of bacterial strains
biology

Presentation. Abstract.

Under the mentorship of Henrique De Paoli , I engineered a strain of E. coli with “switch-like” control over its endogenous expression of vectors.

A construct was assembled to integrate this behavior into the bacterial chromosome through a transposon . Using standard molecular biology benchwork - Assembly PCR, gel electrophoresis, and Gibson assembly - library of novel strains was gathered.

Though the work is ongoing under the Synthetic Biology Informatics group at JBEI , initial results were promising, showing marked increase in host vector production when induced.
